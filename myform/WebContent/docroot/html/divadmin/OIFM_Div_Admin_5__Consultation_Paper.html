Consultation Paper 

 

My Forum allows administrators to publish papers on policies and issues and get feedback from users regarding the paper. It is possible to publish the paper and get feedback only from a group of people. 

 

a.  Click on the “Consultation Paper” button on the left panel of the admin main page.

 

b.   The “Division List” page will show list of divisions who have created a consultation paper. Select a division to display list of consultation papers from the division or click on the “View All Consultation Papers” button to display all consultation papers

 

b.  The “Consultation Paper List” page will show list of consultation papers grouped by categories. 

 

 

1.  Category 

 

1.1.  Create Category 

 

a.  Click on the “Create Category” button 

 

b.  In the “Create Category” page, enter the category name. Click on “Save” button to create the category. NOTE: System does not allow duplicate category name. 

 

 

 

1.2.  Modify Category 

 

a.  Click on the category name 

 

b.  In the “View / Modify Consultation Category” page, modify the category name. Click on “Save” button to save modifications made. NOTE: System does not allow duplicate category name. 

 

 

 

1.3.  Delete Category 

 

a.  Click on the category name 

 

b.  In the “View / Modify Consultation Paper” page, click on the “Delete” button to delete the category. NOTE: System does not allow a category to be deleted if the category still has papers under it. 

 

 

2.  Paper 

 

2.1.  Create Paper 

 

Click on the “Create Paper” button 

 

2.1.1.  Paper Details 

 

In the “Create Consultation Paper” page, enter the details of the paper. The attached paper must be in PDF format. If “No” is selected for the “Would this consultation paper be open to all users ?” question, only users from the selected groups and users with matching interest tags (if “Yes” is selected for the “Would you like to open the paper to other users who have expressed interest in the issues relevant to this paper?” question) will have access to the consultation paper. Click on “Save” button to create the paper without adding questions. Click on “Save and Add Question” button to create the paper and add questions. NOTE: System does not allow duplicate paper title under same category 

 

 

 

2.1.2.  Create Question 

 

a.  After clicking on the “Save and Add Question” button, click on “Add Question” button to open the “New Question” window. 

 

 

b.  Enter the question and answers. Click on “Save” button to create the question. 

 

 

2.2.  Modify Paper 

 

Click on the consultation paper title you wish to modify from the Consultation paper-listing page. 

 

2.2.1.  Modify Paper Details 

 

In the “View / Modify Consultation Paper” page, make necessary modifications. If “No” is selected for the “Would this consultation paper be open to all users ?” question, only users from the selected groups and users with matching interest tags (if “Yes” is selected for the “Would you like to open the paper to other users who have expressed interest in the issues relevant to this paper?” question) will have access to the consultation paper. Click on “Save” button to save modifications made. NOTE: System does not allow duplicate consultation paper title under same category 

 

 

2.2.2.  Consultation Paper Questions 

 

2.2.2.1.  Add Question 

 

a.  From the “View / Modify Consultation Paper” page, click on the “Add Question” button to open the “New Question” window. 

 

 

b.  Enter the question and answers. Click on “Save” button to add a new question. 

 

2.2.2.2.  Modify Question 

 

a.  From the “View / Modify Consultation Paper” page, click on the question to open a pop up window to modify the question. 

 

 

b.  Click on “Save” button to save modifications made. 

 

2.2.2.3.  Remove Question 

 

From the “View / Modify Consultation Paper” page, click on the “Remove” button for the corresponding question. NOTE: System does not allow a question to be deleted if the paper is currently active. 

 

 

2.3  Delete Paper 

 

a.  Click on the consultation paper title 

 

b.  Click on the “Delete” button to delete the consultation paper. NOTE: System does not allow a paper to be deleted if the paper is currently active. An active paper is paper in its active period and published in website. 

 

 

2.4  Publish Consultation Paper 

 

After the period of a paper expired, it is possible to publish the summary of the result from the feedbacks. 

 

a.  Click on the consultation paper title. 

 

b.  Click on the “Publish Consultation Paper” button. NOTE: System does not allow publishing an active paper. 

 

c.  In the “Publish Consultation Results” page, enter the details for publishing the consultation paper feedback result. The attached summary file must be in PDF format. 

 

 

 

2.5  Export Paper 

 

a.  Click on the consultation paper title. 

 

b.  Click on “Export Consultation Paper” button on the bottom part of the screen. System will open the exported paper in Microsoft Excel. You can save or open in browser.
