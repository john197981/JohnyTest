About ASM  

 

The About ASM module allows the admin to maintain questions and answers regarding the ASM module.

 

a. Click on "About ASM" button from the left panel of the admin main page

 

b. The "About ASM" screen will display the list of topics which have been created previously.

 

 

1.  Add New Topic 

 

a. Click on "Add New" link at the top right corner of the "About ASM" screen.

 

 

b.  Enter the topic and explanation.

 

c.  Click on "Save" button to save the topic 

 

2.  Edit Topic 

 

a.  On the About ASM list page, click on the topic.

 

 

b.  Make necessary changes. Click on "Save" button to save the changes.

 

3.  Delete Topic 

 

When in Edit mode, click on "Delete" button to delete the topic.
