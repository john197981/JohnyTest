Consultation Paper 

 

Consultation papers are published by the administrators to get feedbacks on various policies and issues. 

 

a.  Click on "Consultation" tab on the top panel of the screen 

 

b.  The "Consultation Papers" main page is divided into 3 sections: 

 

           * Announcement 

 

           * Current Consultation Papers 

 

           * Past Consultation Papers 

 

 

 

1.  Participate in Consultation Paper 

 

a.  Click on the consultation paper title on the Current Papers section 

 

b.  On the "Consultation Paper Feedback" page, the details of the consultation paper are displayed. The attached consultation paper, if available, can be downloaded and viewed. 

 

 

c.  Answer to questions (if available) and provide feedback. Click on "Save as Draft" to save the feedback as draft. Feedbacks saved as draft are not yet submitted and therefore still can be edited. Click on "Submit" button to submit the feedback. Once the feedback has been submitted, it cannot be edited. 

 

2.  View Past Consultation Paper Summary 

 

Once the period of a consultation paper expired, administrators can publish the result and summary of the feedbacks. Published consultation paper will be listed in the Past Papers section in the "Consultation Paper" main page. 

 

a.  Click on the consultation paper title on the Past Papers section 

 

b.  The "Past Paper" page will display the details of the published consultation paper. 

 

 

3.  Alert Friend 

 

The Alert Friend function is used to alert another user on a consultation paper. 

 

a.  Click on the "Alert Friend" button on "Consultation Paper Feedback" page or "Past Paper" page to open the "Alert Friend" pop up window 

 

 

b.  Enter the recipients email address. Click on "Send" button to send the email.
