Audit Trail 

 

Audit Trail displays trails of moderations on threads and postings made by administrators. 

 

a.  Click on "Audit Trail" button from left panel of the admin main page to open the "Search Audit Trail Transactions" page 

 

 

b.  Select transaction type and enter transaction date. Click on "Search" button to search for audit transactions. 

 

c.  The search result will show trail of audit transactions of the selected type on the period of time.
