Survey 

 

My Forum allows divisions to conduct surveys to get feedback on a particular subject in a structured manner. It is possible to publish surveys and get feedback from a group of people. 

 

a.  Click on "Survey" button on the left panel of the admin main page 

 

b.  The "Division List" page will list divisions who had conducted a survey. Click on a division name to display the list of surveys conducted by the division, or click on "View All Surveys" button to display all surveys.

 

c.  The "Survey List" page will list surveys. 

 

 

 

1.  Create Survey 

 

Click on the "Create Survey" button 

 

1.1.  Survey Details 

 

In the "Create Survey" page, enter the details of the survey. If "No" is selected for the "Would this survey be open to all users ?" question, only users from the selected groups and users with matching interest tags (if "Yes" is selected for the "Would you like to open the survey to other users who have expressed interest in the issues relevant to this survey?" question) will have access to the survey. Click on "Save" to create the survey and return to the "Survey List" page. Click on "Save & Next" to create the survey and go to the "Section" page to create and modify sections. 

 

 

 

1.2.  Create Section 

 

a.  In the "Section" page, enter the details of the section. Click on "Save" button to create the section. 

 

 

b.  Click on "Next >>" button to go to "Section & Questions" page to create questions. 

 

 

1.3.  Create Question 

 

a.  The "Section & Questions" page will list the hierarchy of sections and questions 

 

 

b.  Click on the section (or sub-section) name to open the "Create / Modify Question" pop up window. 

 

 

c.  Enter the details of the question. Click on "Save" button to create the question. 

 

 

1.4.  Publish Survey in Website 

 

When creating survey, the survey will be published in website only after clicking on the "Publish in Website" button on the "Survey & Questions" page or check the "Publish on Web" checkbox in the "View / Modify Survey" page.

 

2.  Modify Survey 

 

Click on the survey name 

 

2.1.  Modify Survey Details 

 

In the "View / Modify Survey" page, make necessary modifications. Check the "Publish on Web" checkbox to publish the survey. Click on "Save" button to save the modifications made and return to "Survey List" page. Click on "Save & Next" button to save the modifications made and go the "Section" page. Click on "Sections" tab to go to "Section" page without saving. 

 

 

 

2.2.  Sections 

 

2.2.1.  Create Section 

 

In the "Section" page, enter the details of the section. Click on "Save" button to create new sections. 

 

 

 

2.2.2.  Modify Section 

 

a.  In the "Section" page, click on the section (or sub-section) name 

 

b.  The details of the selected section will be displayed in the same page. Make necessary modifications and click on "Save" button to same modifications made. To exit the editing mode and create new section, click on the "Add New Section" button. 

 

 

 

2.2.3.  Delete Section 

 

a.  In the "Section" page, click on the name of the section (or sub-section). 

 

b.  Click on "Delete" button to delete the section. All questions under that section will also deleted. NOTE: System does not allow a section to be deleted when the survey is currently active. 

 

2.3.  Questions 

 

2.3.1.  Create New Questions 

 

a.  The "Section & Questions" page will list the hierarchy of sections and questions 

 

 

 

b.  Click on the section (or sub-section) name to open the "Create / Modify Question" pop up window. 

 

 

 

c.  Enter the details of the question. Click on "Save" button to create a new question. 

 

2.3.2.  Modify Question 

 

a.  On the "Section & Questions" page, click on the question name to open the "Create / Modify Question" 

 
b.  Make necessary modification to the question. Click on "Save" button to save modifications made. 

 

2.3.3.  Delete Question 

 

a.  On the "Section & Questions" page, click on the question name to open the "Create / Modify Question" pop up window 

 

b.  Click on "Delete" button to delete the question. NOTE: System does not allow a question to be deleted if the survey is currently active. 

 

 

3.  Delete Survey 

 

a.  Click on the survey name 

 

b.  Click on "Delete" button to delete the survey. NOTE: System does not allow a survey to be deleted when the survey is currently active 

 


4. Publish Survey Result

After the period of the survey expired, it is possible to publish the result and summary of the survey in the website. 

 

a.  On the "View / Modify Survey" page, click on the "Publish Survey Result" button. NOTE: System does not allow publishing a survey when the survey is currently active 

 

b.  In the "Publish Survey" page, enter the summary and survey result file. The attached file must be in PDF format. 

 

 

 

5.  Export Survey 

 

On the "View / Modify Survey" page, click on the "Export" button. The system will export the survey in Microsoft Excel format. 

 

6.  Create Temporary User Batch 

 

It is possible to invite non-GDS users to participate in the survey. For this purpose, the system can generate temporary user Ids. When a user logs in using a temporary user ID, the user will be automatically redirected to the corresponding survey. After the user submitted the survey response, system will automatically disable the user ID. The user also will not be able to browse another modules. 

 

a.  From the "Survey List" page, click on the "Create User IDs" 

 

b.  On the "Temp User Creation", select the survey and enter the number of user IDs to be generated. Click on "Generate IDs" button to generate the number of user IDs for the selected survey. 

 

 

 

c.  Temporary User IDs are created in batches. So every time a number of temporary users IDs created for a survey, they will be created on a new different batch than the previous. 

 

7.  Remove Temporary User Batch 

 

a.  On the "Temp Users Creation" page, select the survey and the batch number 

 

b.  Click on "Remove" button to remove the selected temporary user batch. 

 

8.  Export Temporary User Batch 

 

a.  On the "Temp Users Creation" page, select the survey and the batch number. 

 

b.  Click on "Export User ID" button to export the list of user IDs and password from the selected batch. The list of temporary user IDs will be exported in Microsoft Excel format.
