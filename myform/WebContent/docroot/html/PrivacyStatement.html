Privacy Statement



We respect the need for confidentiality in providing genuine and sincere views and comments. To encourage free-flowing dialogue within the Discussion Forum, users' personal information, other than the hobbies and their personalised nicknames, will not be available to Moderators or any other users.
As we might need to contact respondents in Surveys and Consultation Paper, only statutory names, not nicknames, will be reflected in Survey and Consultation Paper responses. Identities are only known to the Main Administrators of My Forum , who are staff of MOE Corporate Communications Division. We will NOT share identities with other users, except where follow-up action is needed for users who contravene the Terms of Use.

To safeguard all personal data, electronic storage and transmission of such data are secured with appropriate security technologies.
